The Duality Of Bliss, Genre-less, Liquid, Lucid, Happy, Splattered with changes, An Organic, Un-orthadox Circuit Euphoria. Entendre Entendre Lends itself to all types of sound, Striving to find Harmony In every Musical Perspective. Through EE's 6 year musical Journey, it has evolved with multiple line up changes including collaborations with many different artists. Everything from saxophone, trumpet and flute players to belly dancers and fire spinners. Entendre Entendre's purpose is to find elegance in Chaos. Taking you down a Psychedelic Wormhole to experience all Genres like you never have before.

A Hundred Songs Later, A Couple Hundred more Forgotten, A Hundred or so Shows Played, A Tour to the East Cost, And a Relocation from the Mid-West to their New found home in Portland, Oregon

Entendre Entendre is more than a band. Entendre Entendre is an Idea, an Energy, Displaying itself through analog and digital vibrational frequencies Manipulated into masterpieces of Sound.
